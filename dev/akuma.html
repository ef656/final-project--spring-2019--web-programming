Digital Design and Usability

Info –

The Akuma Shrimp Burger is named after “Akuma” which is Japanese for devil. This is due to the fact that this delicacy is a Japanese-style, double stacked, extra spicy challenge burger. The idea is that the burger has a secret sauce that is 200 times more spicy than Tabasco sauce and thus in- dividual challengers have 10 minutes to devour the burger. Successful chal- lengers will have obvious bragging rights, a picture posted on the Akuma Burger Wall, and a free t-shirt. Failing participants have to wear a hat of failure for the rest of their stay at the restaurant.
