Info –

Metamorphosis is my honors project. Its aim is to provide resources for undocumented students such as information about scholarships and organizations. A core component of the website is the stories of undocumented undergraduates, graduates, and post-grads as means let other undocumented students know that there is no one straight path to achieve their goals. Metamorphosis' core goal is to let young undocumented students know that there is hope to fulfill their dreams. They shouldn’t pause their dreams because of not having papers.
