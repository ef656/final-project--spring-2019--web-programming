This is a website for Unstoppable, a relatively new ice cream parlour, focused on serving delicious, in-the-house made ice creams and most importantly women empowerment. We believe that the empowerment, journey to liberation, and improvement of women's social, health, and political status is crucial, which is why 40% of our sales each month go to a women-oriented organizations. We believe that this is just a small stepping stone to help women, which is why we encourage our cutomers to get involved with the organizations as well.
